Interest Guidelines and Calculations

Learn how interest is calculated based on the loan amount, duration, and loan type.

Interest for Personal Loans is 15% for 7 days and prorated for longer durations. Emergency Loans carry a 20% annual interest rate, while Business Loans carry a 25% annual interest rate.

Late fees of KES 20 apply per day after the 7th day of the loan's duration.
