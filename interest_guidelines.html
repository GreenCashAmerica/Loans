Nova Prime - Interest Guidelines

Below are detailed interest rates and fee structures applicable to all loan types offered by Nova Prime. These rates comply with Central Bank of Kenya (CBK) regulations and the Microfinance Act.

Loan Types & Interest Rates

  • Personal Loan: 15% per annum
  • Emergency Loan: 20% per annum
  • Business Loan: 25% per annum

Additional Fees

  • Excise Duty: 20% of the interest amount
  • Facilitation Fee: 1% of the loan principal
  • M-PESA Transaction Fee: Applied as per Safaricom tariffs

Repayment Periods

  • 7 Days
  • 14 Days
  • 30 Days

Choose your preferred duration during application. Interest is calculated proportionally to the loan period selected.

Late Repayment Fees

If payment is delayed beyond the agreed repayment date, a penalty of KES 50 per day will apply until the loan is settled.

Non-payment may lead to listing with licensed CRBs and potential legal action under the Kenyan Banking Act and CRB Regulations (2020).
